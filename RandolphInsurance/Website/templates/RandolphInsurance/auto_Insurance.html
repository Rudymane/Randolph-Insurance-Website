Auto Insurance

Having the proper auto insurance is essential to minimizing your risk when you are on the road. Make sure you and your friends/family are protected in the event of an accident. Take a look at the required coverages and feel free to ask us questions!

Bodily Injury Liability/ Personal Property

In the event of an at-fault accident, this coverage will cover the bodily injury and property damages of the non at-fault party.


Uninsured/Underinsured Bodily Injury

Protect yourself and passengers from uninsured and or underinsured drivers. If you are hit by a driver without sufficient coverages, this coverage will cover your bodily injury costs.

First Party Benefits

These benefits include your medical payment limit, income loss, accidental death, and funeral benefit.



Full Tort/ Limited Tort

Determine whether you want the option to go after more money than what a the claims amount payed out.



Comprehensive Coverage

Protect your vehicle against all claims not collision, such as deer, glass, weather, etc.




Collision Coverage

Protect your vehicle againsta all collision claims, such as a car accident with another vehcile, collision with an object, car damage from pothole, etc.
